_Simple Weapon (Finesse, Light, Thrown (range 20/60), uncommon (requires attunement)_
**Damage:** 1d4 Piercing
**Mastery**: Nick
**Weight**: 1 lb.
**Cost**: 20 GP. 

**Traits**:
- **Silent Step**: While wielding this dagger, your footfalls are muffled by a subtle enchantment.  
You gain **advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks** made in **natural environments** (such as forests, swamps, caves, etc.).
- **Hunters Mark**:  (1 / Long rest): When **reducing a creature to 0 HP** using this dagger, you may choose another creature you can see within **60 feet** , you then have **advantage on attack rolls against that creature** for your next **three attacks**.

This elegantly curved dagger bears a blade of shimmering green steel etched with wood elf script. Its hilt is made of antler wrapped in sun-dried sinew, the balance perfect, the edge  sharp and maintained. When drawn, it briefly smells of moss and loam. It is a blade meant for silent strikes and blood never seems to stick to it.
