## Ravenbone Totem 
 *Wondrous item, uncommon (magical, Requires Attunement)* 
 
**Cost**: 50 GP
**Weight**:  --
 **Traits:**
 - **Once per long rest**, when you are reduced to **0 hit points** but not killed outright, you may choose to **make your** first death saving throw with advantage**.
 
 Carved from a raven’s leg bone and etched with worn tribal markings, this totem is strung on an old leather cord. A braid of white hair wraps the top like thread. It is light in the hand, but heavy with memory.
